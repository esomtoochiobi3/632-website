My Background

Hello to anyone that may end up on this page! My name is Esom Too-Chiobi and I am a Master's student studying Computer Engineering at Texas A&M. I am currently working on my thesis, which focuses on music information retrieval (MIR).

Interests in Accessibility

I have a deep interest in accessible computing because I believe it's necessary that we always remember everyone that interacts with the technology. My main interest is in universally designed interfaces that adapt to various user needs.

Key Topics of Interest

  • Flexible Design Approaches
  • Simple & Intuitive Interfaces
  • Perceptible Information Design
